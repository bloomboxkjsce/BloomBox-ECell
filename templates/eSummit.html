Sociathon


 15th April, 2022    1:00 pm


A competition for a cause. Unlike other competitions where the participation is for winning and gaining experience, this competition will also promote, encourage and foster the idea of social entrepreneurship which is defined as an approach by individuals, groups, start-up companies or entrepreneurs, in which they develop, fund and implement solutions to social, cultural, or environmental issues. In this competition, the participants are expected to come up with an idea that resolves an issue pertaining to social entrepreneurship

Sociathon

It will start with a speaker/panel session where you will understand everything about social entrepreneurship, its WHY, WHEN, WHAT, WHERE, HOW, and FOR. This knowledge will help you in all the rounds of the competition. You will get an opportunity to apply this knowledge to practical use through this competition. It will consist of 2 rounds on only one single domain - Social entrepreneurship

  • Round 1 - You have to decide on a problem, find a solution, prepare a business plan around it, show the impact and everything that you can think of.
  • Round 2 - Selected teams will be given problem statements by us 30-45 min before. You then have to speak about the problem, ideated solution, approach, impact, etc for around 2-3 minutes.
Results will be announced by judges later on!

Frequently Asked Questions



What is E-Summit?

BloomBox is here with the second edition of E-Summit of KJSCE to promote Entrepreneurship among young minds.

Who can be a part of this E-Summit?

All undergrads can participate in the Summit.

Is there any registration fees for any event of E-Summit?

No, there's no registration fees for any of the event as we are a Non-Profit Organization.

Is there a limit on number of events once can participate in?

No, there's no limit on the number of events one can participate in.

Should all team members be of same college for any event?

No, there's no such necessity but all the team members should be undergrads.

On what platforms will the events be held on?

Events will be held on various platforms like Google Meet, Zoom and Microsoft Teams.

Will the events be held in offline or online mode?

The entire E-Summit will be held in an online mode apart from the Pre-Event which will be held in hybrid mode.
